  • Last Week in the Sigma Chapter

    43rd Annual Gamma Iota Sigma Management Conference

    October 3rd, 2014

    The Sigma Chapter won five Chapter Awards, four Black and Gold Awards and five Individual Scholarship Awards at the Annual Management Conference co-hosted by the Zeta Chapter at the University of North Texas. The Sigma Chapter was also recognized as a Superior Chapter along with three other chapters.

    Temple University Cleanup

    September 28, 2014

    Gamma Iota Sigma joined forces with Net Impact to clean up the campus and surrounding areas. The volunteers were able to collect upwards of 30 bags of trash within an hour!
